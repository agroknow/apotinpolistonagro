Presenting an "AGRICULTURAL BUSINESS ECOSYSTEM"

A NEW REALITY

A unique opportunity is given to Greek people, within this economic crisis, to return or invest in the primary sector.
A return that will be accompanied by all the necessary tools that in order to be successful.


THE REASON BEHIND THE EVENT

The occupation, via individual or joint agricultural ventures, of a large and extremely diverse group of people, accumulated knowledge,
experiences and ideas. Simultaneously, the group realized - and continues to realize - that rural entrepreneurship has
tremendous potential if treated holistically. Nowadays, there is a need to share all these ideas and experiences aiming not only
at informing people, but also at engaging new stakeholders in existing or new business ventures.


THE EVENT HAS BEEN DESIGNED TO:


• bring us into contact with agricultural life and experiences
• highlight specific projects that are already “running”
• show how to access all the necessary tools for successful
engagement with rural entrepreneurship
• constitute an agri-business hub between farmers, entrepreneurs and investors (AgroHub)
• highlight a group of young farmers, which will come under the umbrella of "City to farm"
within the context of an agricultural incubator


THE EVENT IS ADDRESSED TO:

Young growers who want to make the transition from town to countryside.
Existing growers interested in participating in some of the projects.
Investors who wish to invest in the primary or secondary sector.
Enterprises of the agri-food sector.

Ο "πιλοτικός αγρός" μας περιμένει να τον επισκεφθούμε. Φακή, ιπποφαές, ζέα και σιτάρι είναι κάποιες απο τις καλλιέργειες

Στα πλαίσια της εκδήλωσης, μια ομάδα 10 νέων αγροτών θα αναδειχθεί και θα μπει υπό την ομπρέλλα του project "Απο Την Πόλη Στον Αγρό"
